Henry Howard-Jenkins

henry [at] henryhj [dot] com LinkedIn / GitHub / Scholar


I am a Research Scientist at Meta Reality Labs Research. Before this, I did my DPhil in the Active Vision Laboratory (AVL) at the University of Oxford, where I was supervised by Victor Prisacariu. My research focused on the application of machine learning to 3D egocentric vision, particularly object and scene reconstruction. I hold an MEng in Engineering Science from the University of Oxford, graduating with First Class Honours.
