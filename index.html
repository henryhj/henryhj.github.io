I am a third year DPhil candidate in the Active Vision Laboratory (AVL) at the University of Oxford. My supervisors are Victor Prisacariu and David Murray. My research projects cover a variety of topics, but I am particularly interested in the application of machine learning to 3D vision.

Before starting my DPhil, I attained a MEng in Engineering Science from the University of Oxford, graduating with First Class Honours.
